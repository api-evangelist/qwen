GITHUB HUGGING FACE MODELSCOPE DEMO DISCORD Introduction In this blog, we delve into the details of our latest Qwen2.5 series language models. We have developed a range of decoder-only dense models, with seven of them open-sourced, spanning from 0.5B to 72B parameters. Our research indicates a significant interest among users in models within the 10-30B range for production use, as well as 3B models for mobile applications.
